## Description
SermonFlow is a distraction-free note-taking app designed for the sanctuary. Capture your spiritual insights with a clean, parchment-inspired timeline that keeps your reflections organized and accessible.

Key Features:
- **Vertical Timeline**: Effortlessly scroll through your spiritual journey.
- **Scripture Engine**: Quick offline access to the World English Bible (WEB) for verse lookup.
- **Life Application Toggle**: Separate tactical "to-dos" from deep theological reflections.
- **Date Scrubber**: Quickly navigate to notes from specific Sundays or services.
- **Privacy First**: Secure, anonymous backup via Firebase ensures your notes are yours alone.
