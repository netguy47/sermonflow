# Privacy Policy for SermonFlow
**Effective Date: January 30, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
SermonFlow ("we," "us," or "our") is a minimalist church note-taking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and your spiritual reflections.

## 2. Data Storage & Firebase
SermonFlow uses Firebase (Google Cloud) for data persistence and synchronization.
* **Anonymous Authentication:** We use anonymous authentication to ensure your notes are private to your device without requiring personal email or identity data upfront.
* **Notes Content:** Your notes are stored securely in Firestore and are only accessible by your authenticated device.
* **Offline Persistence:** Notes are cached locally on your device for offline use.

You have the right to delete your notes at any time. Since we use anonymous authentication, your data is tied to your device signature.
